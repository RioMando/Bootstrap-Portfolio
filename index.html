About Me

I was born in Torreon, Mexico. "The city that defeated the desert" is the land of the Santos Laguna soccer team, and home of some of the bravest, corageous, and hard working women and men in earth.

I studied Electronic Engineering in the La Laguna Technological Institute and I have a Masters degree in Planning and Informatic Systems by the Iberoamericana University Torreon. And I have worked in several sectors, as education, manufacturing, general services, etc.

I'm passionate aboout robotics, and to help kiddos from elementary level to discover their potential throughout this fascinating "creatures" and to believe that they can achieve wathever they want if they have a dream and are willing to follow it.

I enjoy the outdoors, mountain biking (totally amateur, BTW), reading, baking bread and try new foods: to cooke them as well as to try them.
